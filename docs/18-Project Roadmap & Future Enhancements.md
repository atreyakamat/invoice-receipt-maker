# 1. Vision

To become the leading AI-powered invoice intelligence platform that automates document processing, reduces manual accounting work, and enables businesses of every size to manage financial documents with speed, accuracy, and confidence.

---

# 2. Mission

Our mission is to eliminate repetitive invoice processing by combining OCR, artificial intelligence, automation, and intuitive user experience into a secure, scalable SaaS platform.

# 12. Risk Register

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|

| Database growth | Medium | Partitioning & archiving |

| Unexpected cloud costs | Medium | Budgets & monitoring |
